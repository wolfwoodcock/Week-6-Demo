Wolf Woodcock

Composer/Instrumentalist

About

I am a composer and multi-instrumentalist based out of Los Angeles, California.

I generally work in the field of controled improvistation spanning from live performances to recordings and film scores. I also write songs that I perform with a live band.

I am currently finishing my last year as an undergraduate composer at CalArts.

Experience

  • Composition for media 2016-present

    I have scored animated and live action works by students and independent filmmakers.

  • A&R for record labels 2015-present

    I have done A&R work for two record labels, including one that I started myself. I find bands performing interesting music and help facilitate getting the band signed to a record label.

  • Session and live musician 2013-present

    I have performed with a variety of instruments on albums made by my own projects as well as others' recording projects. I also have toured throughout the US and Europe with three professional groups

Projects

  • Inspector Margaret (2018)

    A score for a senior thesis animation by Scottie Harvey.

  • Cove (2017)

    My latest EP of songs written for a six piece ensemble.

  • Glitch (2017)

    A composition premiered by The Ensemble at CalArts
